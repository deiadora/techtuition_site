who this is for

Founders. And their teams.

The on-ramp is for founders who recognise something in these field notes — who have sensed the fractal architecture underneath the work but haven't had language or geometry for it yet. It is for the people building in the field, not studying it from outside.

Steps 0 through 3 are self-paced. No cohort required, no application, no discovery call. You work through them when you're ready, at your own pace, and the playbook you build is yours to keep and use regardless of what comes next.

Steps 4 through 9 — wave, direction, transmission, expression, structure, source — live inside the facilitated cohort. They require the field that live, held group work generates. Entry is by conversation, not application. If the pattern recognises itself after Step 3, the next move is to write.
