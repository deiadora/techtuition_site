what this is

The teaching that was always the framework.

Techtuition is built on a multiscalar framework — individual, team, organisation — and a quantum sequence (0–9) that maps to the body, the group, and the collective. It applies at every scale because it describes the actual architecture of coherent systems, not a methodology invented for the workplace.

The practical output: operations, collaboration, and governance patterns and rituals that cohere rather than extract. Teams stop running on nineteenth-century Newtonian assumptions and start running on the physics of how complexity actually organises itself.

The foundational book — Quantum Currency: Field Research on Human Coherence — is the formal statement of what four years of workshops and thirteen years of field research produced. The workshop is where it becomes operational.
